About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Georgia Institute of Technology, where I am advised by Prof. Eva Dyer. My areas of interest are Self-Supervised Learning and Large-Scale Learning. I like working with challenging datasets and am actively working on developing deep learning methods for computational-neuroscience, graphs, and timeseries. I did my undergrad in ECE from the Indian Institute of Technology - Roorkee. Before diving into machine learning research, I used to work as a chip-design engineer.
